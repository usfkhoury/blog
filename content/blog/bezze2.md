# Steps

1. **11:00 AM (2h30m)** Ghasil bi may mn jat la jat la tondaf mayetoun mn l wa7el wl wara2 (x5)
2. **1:17 PM** **(1h)**
    1. 3abe nos l tanjra <u>may msa23a bala mele7 bala chi</u> (eza l may 3am teghle deghre bi dobbo 3a baytoun)
    2. 3abbiya bezze2 bass khalliyoun yotla3o abl ma tdawwe <u>nar kharife</u> 3layoun hek bi mouto henne w barra
    3. Lamma ymouto awwe chwe l nar, w lamma ytal3o raghwe w ybalcho yeghlo taffe l nar
    4. Fta7 l may 3layoun chwe la yondafo chway w trou7 l raghwe
    5. Krezoun bi mosfeye, cha2chi2 l tanjra, cha2che2oun chwe bl mosfeye
    6. 3id l steps x1→2 marrat ma3 rachit mele7 kl marra w <u>3a nar awiyye</u>
3. **2:10 PM** **(3h)** (x2→3 aw la ybatlo yergho ktir w tsir mayetoun ndife nesbiyan)

    Nafs tari2it l sale2 wl tondif bass <u>3a nar khafife</u> la yeghlo w ma3:

        - mele7 khechin
        - 1→3 war2it ghar
        - 1/2 2osbi3 2erfe
4. **5:00 PM** **(2h→3h)**

    Nafs tari2it l sale2 la ysiro yotla3o bl curdane bi shoule bass <u>3a nar khafife</u> la yeghlo w ma3:

        - 1/2→1 basle
