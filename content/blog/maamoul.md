## Liquid Prep

1. Dawid l samne wl zebde sawa
2. Khaliyoun yeberdo 3al ekhir
3. Zid nos kamiyit l mazaher wl mawared w trok l be2e la tene nhar

## Dry Prep


Nkhol l t7in wl raweyi7 sawa


## 3ajen 1

1. Frekoun w farfetoun khalliyoun yekhdo ba3ed
2. Ghattiyoun bi war2it naylon lez2a 3a wejjoun bala ma ta2eloun (mafiyoun khamire) la tene nhar l soboh max (12-24 hours)

## Tamer

1. Mlok l tamer la yen3amo
2. Zid 3layoun khbaroun ma3 l zebde l fetra
3. Mlekoun zyede la yekhdo ba3ed
4. Ghatiyou bi war2it naylon w raye7oul la tene nhar
5. Bl 2idek bl may w mlekoun chway
6. Ka3zeloun w soffoun 3a saniye (~14g)

## 3ajen 2

1. Mn ba3ed ma yerte7o zid be2e l mazaher wl mawared w frekoun khalik 3am bt zid mawared w mazaher la ysirometl l 3ajin w yenna2aro balaa yoforto. W 2eza ba3ed ma akhado sir zid mawared mch mazaher la2ann bya3te ta3me morra. Bass ou3a tzid ktir ahsan ma yerkho ktir
2. Fik t7ot 92g water 4g sekar 6g khamire
3. Ballich tek3ik w 7ot nos l khamire bass tbalich tene batch zid l be2e

## Khabez

1. Khbezoun bass la yekhdo nakzit lon 3al wejj w deghre chiloun 200
